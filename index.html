UI Concept. Keep it simple.

The idea of destroying your social profile is scary so F*%K U's design will have to be simple and intuitive to compete with the addictive quality of social media. With only one button as an interface it is as clear and effective as possible.

Destroy yourself. Virtually.

The constant creep of soical media platforms grasp into our private lives has gone on long enough. Without you holding all proprietary control over your data, the only way to claim back some control is to void it. F*%K U provides the capability to disseminate incorrect and misleading information from any of your social platforms which will render your profile an outlier, and of no value to ad agencies or government services.
